## Get Started with Scriptmasters

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    1. Open Scriptmasters dashboard.
    2. Click "New Project".
    3. Name your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first script">
    1. Go to your new project page.
    2. Click "Add Script".
    3. Enter script content or upload a file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize with folders">
    1. Click "New Folder" to create sections.
    2. Drag and drop scripts into folders for better structure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
