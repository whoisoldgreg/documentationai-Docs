## Creating a Project

Start your documentation in Scriptmasters in a few steps:

1. Log in to your Scriptmasters dashboard.
2. Click "New Project" at the top right.
3. Enter your project name and description.
4. Choose a visibility option (public or private).
5. Click "Create" to finish.

Your new project will appear on your dashboard. You can now add scripts and organize your documentation easily.
