---
title: Cannot Save Script
description: Resolve script-saving errors in Scriptmasters.
---

## Issue

- Script changes are not saving
- "Failed to save" error appears

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check internet connection">
    Ensure you have a stable connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh your browser">
    Press `Ctrl+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+R` (Mac) to reload the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-authenticate if needed">
    Log out and log back into your Scriptmasters account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Sometimes cached data causes issues.
  </Step>
</Steps>
